Tenoch Huerta

Who?

Tenoch Huerta, famous actor and activist

Why?

It all started with the release of Black Panther: Wakanda Forever. I was never a fan of the movie due to the bizare decision to make a movie about Africans with okay two actual Africans in it (WEIRD) But then I saw this tweet that highlighted the romantic undertones of their story and exchanges. That's when I saw the clips people were getting from the movie. Not only did I see the romance but I saw HIM looking fionne as hell in those native american garb. I then looked into his other movies and saw his work in activism for black & brown peoples. I was HOOKED

What?

I'm sure you are wondering what having Tenoch Huerta as a hobby means. It means creating edits of all his work on Tiktok, writing essays in his honour on reddit, getting into twitter wars about him, posting picture of him on pinterest and instagram. It entails reading his book that I didn't like and giving it a five star review and watching the movies of his that actually suck but only telling the fanclub that.
